Introduction

Renewable energy is collected from natural sources such as sunlight, wind, and organic matter. These sources replenish themselves and help reduce environmental pollution.

Types of Renewable Energy

Biofuel

Biofuel is produced from organic materials such as plants and agricultural waste. It serves as an eco-friendly alternative to fossil fuels.

Solar Energy

Solar energy captures the sunlight and converts it into electricity. It is one of the most widely used clean energy sources.

Wind Energy

Wind turbines convert wind movement into usable power. It is efficient, renewable, and widely accessible.
